About

This site was created by Elsa Ko in June of 2023 as a practical exercise in machine learning for the DevLabs program in the CRA! This topic was selected both as a hobby project and because two types of ML approaches could be used - clustering for unsupervised and classification for supervised learning.

Originally, the goal was to create a simple application to practice deploying a model to a practical environment. However, as it grew, a bunch of other cool ideas sort of jumped along too, becoming what it is now.
The whole thing ended up being an exercise in design, javascript, bootstrap, and of course, machine learning.
